Design Automation Design cost-optimized, quotable cloud and storage solutions using real workload assessment data.

  • Mathematical Optimization

    Generate cost-optimized, quotable solutions while meeting design policies and workload requirements.

  • Design Collaboration

    Real-time design collaboration for cross-functional teams building cloud and storage solutions.

  • Workload Placement

    Optimize workload placement across compute and storage tiers to minimize solution costs.

  • Capacity Planning

    Right-size compute and storage resource pools using real workload profiles.

Collaborative CPQ Collaborate with your team, vendors and distribution partners to configure, price and quote cloud and storage infrastructure solutions.

  • Shared Workspace

    Share your client engagement with colleagues so that everyone can collaborate and contribute.

  • Drag-and-Drop

    Build and modify solutions using an intuitive drag-and-drop interface that anyone can use.

  • Reports and Diagrams

    Generate rich client deliverables with solution diagrams and supporting product information.

  • BoM Generation

    Generate bills of materials using the latest pricing and promotions in your local currency.

Hardware Asset Intelligence Normalize and enrich raw hardware asset data with actionable business metrics to demonstrate the value of a technology refresh.

  • Data Normalization

    Normalize server makes and models, CPU and operating system data.

  • Data Enrichment

    Age

    Power consumption

    Warranty remaining

    Carbon footprint

    Rack units used

    Head dissipation

    Form factor

    Power costs
